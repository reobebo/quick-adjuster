What is an adjuster?

We represent the policy holders and acts as advocates for policy holders. Insurance companies only cover a fraction of repair expenses after losses. A PA is state-licensed, insurance professional who can help you settle an insurance claim. A PA works on behalf of the policy holder, NOT the insurance company. Pas are involved in the process of appraising and negotiating a claim. We adequately handle and administer the construction procedure.
